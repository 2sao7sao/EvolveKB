# Refund Policy Demo Source

Customers may request a refund within 14 days of purchase if the product has not
been used in production. Refunds after 14 days require manager approval.

Enterprise contracts with custom payment terms must be routed to finance before
any customer-facing refund commitment is made.

Support agents may draft a refund explanation, but they must not execute the
refund or promise a refund until the order state, contract terms, and approval
status are verified.

If evidence is missing, the assistant must say which evidence is missing and
route the case to the right owner instead of guessing.
